Hotdog

Description

Very simple to make! A beautiful grille sausage in a bun with sauce of your liking.

Ingredients

  • 1 sausage (meat/pork)
  • 1 hotdog bun
  • Sauce of your liking
  • Toppings of your likinh (salad/cheese/jalapenos

Steps

  1. Grill sauce on pan until it is cooked all the way through.
  2. Toast bun (on pan or with toaster).
  3. Put sausage between the hotdog buns.
  4. Add toppings of your choice on top of sausage.
  5. Add sauce of your choice over hotdog.
