Mac and Cheese

Description

Macaroni pasta covered in creamy cheese sauce. Easy to cook in a short time!

Ingredients

  • 300g macaroni
  • 30g butter
  • 25g flour
  • 500ml milk
  • 200g grated cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220C
  2. Cook macaroni until nearly done (if usually cooked for 10 minutes, cook for 8 minutes).
  3. Melt butter on low heat for cheese sauce. When butter is foaming, add flower and mix in. Cook for 2 minutes while stirring.
  4. Gradually add milk while stirring sauce. Stir until mixture is smooths and no lumps.
  5. When cheese sauce is ready, add salt and paper to your taste.
  6. Drain nearly all the water from the pasta (leave a little).
  7. Add pasta to the cheese sauce and stir.
  8. Pour pasta and cheese sauce in ovenproof tray. Bake in oven for 10 minutes.
